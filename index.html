🌟 Our Mission & Vision

Mission

A world where every child grows in peace, safety, and dignity—nurtured with love, protected from harm, and given the chance to thrive.

Vision

“Let’s Pray for Children” exists to unite people in faith, generosity, and action—raising vital funds and lifting heartfelt prayers to support vulnerable children.
